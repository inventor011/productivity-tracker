**Products Descriptions:**

**Weekly Tracker:** This was the first product and it was made months a back maybe 6 to 7 months back and its origin story is simple, I wanted to keep track of what did i got done in a week so I made it.

Its simple, whatever tasks I used to do in a day I wrote it down and assigned it a point between 0 to 2. If it was simple one then between 0 to 1 and if the task was extraordinary and I loved it then it was assigned the number 2.

At the end of the week, you get the report of all the tasks done in a downloadable MD file or a text file and also points out of 7 as each day is defined to have a point of 1, if you assign it more than 1 point then it must have been a extraordinary day to remember.

On basis of the points a percentage is calculated which gets auto updated in the Progress tab and you can see it and also download a line or bar graph for it.

You can decide your own passing percentage, set your own threshold in the Progress tab.

**To-do-list task management:** You all have been using this sort of thing from many time for sure you must be knowing, I wanted something personal of high quality with my own touch rather than the shitty stuffs in the market.

**Questions Ranker:** This is interesting one. Many a time I have many sort of questions to get answers of, you must have been through this situation as well and its definitely not possible to get answers of all and not all are equally important so I needed a place to write it all, all the questions and know which one is more important than the other. That's why I build this, it takes your Gemini API key (freely available in the Google AI Studio) and uses it to rank the questions and rate them on scale of 1 to 10 so it becomes easy for you to decide which question to focus more on and give more time into.

Just paste the API key and enjoy. It automatically runs at a schedule to rank the questions.

**Streak:** Some habits are hard to form and we forget about them easily but they are important and that happened with me as well, so to keep track of a habit and build a streak I build this one.

Simply enter a habit, number of days you wanna follow and then if you follow it on a specific day then log that day or just simply, the streak goes back to zero.

Simple habit tracking and enforcing tool.

**Logbook:** The latest addition and the one of the most important one. We sometimes keep working on a project and don't know how much is done or can't keep track of a project/topic/task. This one is for that.

You log a Project/Task and Subtasks as well, write notes and stuffs and keep track of your Projects and complete them on time.

Out of these 5 stacks, you may use only 1 or 2 or maybe all 5 but I think each one is valuable and helps you in enhancing productivity and tracking your work.
